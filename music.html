ZOMBIE APOCALYPSE

The second album from ZERO heavy metal upstarts ZOMBIE APOCALYPSE:"I got your melancholy, baby", murmurs someone in the studio. And then, a chaos and a relentlessness leave no question that the album sport the mark of the devil. The albums's sound is a snarling summary of extreme metal in 2018, a menacing hodgepodge of blackened crust, hardcore punk and heavy-metal. This particular recipe of unholy crossover has been steadily gaining ground, thanks to Prosthetic Records.

ALBUMS

HOW TO KILL A ZOMBIE

HOW TO KILL A ZOMBIE, the first album, came out one week before September 11th, 2013. It's a lead single, "Less Than Nothing!" famously landed on a Cloudy Channel blacklist of songs to avoid broadcasting in the wake of the attacks on the Worl Trade Center.

"Less Than Nothing!" became a hit all the same, a heavy-metal chimera that crashed unintelligible babbling into the chorus's gorgeous vocal melody. ZERO released the album and remastered and re=released in a variety of formats-from a two-disc reissue with bonus material to two-LP and a CD monstrosity that feautures a hardcover book of photos and liner notes and is stuffed with emergency response prints, patches, and assorted swag to fill Christmas stocking.
